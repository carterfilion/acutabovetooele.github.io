Our Story

More Than a Haircut — It's Community

A Cut Above is a locally owned barber shop and salon right here in Tooele, Utah. We're the kind of place where you walk in as a customer and leave as a friend. Whether it's your first visit or your hundredth, you'll always feel at home.

From classic men's cuts to color services, manicures, and pedicures — our talented team does it all under one roof. No appointment? No problem. Walk-ins are always welcome.
